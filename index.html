Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about AYUVOYAGE

How does AYUVOYAGE verify partner hospitals and retreats?

We conduct thorough due diligence on all partner centers, including verification of certifications, medical licenses, patient reviews, and facility inspections. Only centers meeting our strict quality standards are accepted as partners.

What types of treatments are available through AYUVOYAGE?

We offer Ayurvedic treatments including stress management, mental health therapy, detox, chronic pain management, and general wellness. All provided by certified practitioners.

How long does the process take from inquiry to treatment?

We match you with treatment centers within 48-72 hours. The complete process, including itinerary and travel arrangements, takes 2-4 weeks.

Do you provide support during the treatment period?

Yes, we provide full support throughout your journey. Our team assists with travel logistics, cultural orientation, and is available 24/7 during your stay. We also conduct follow-up consultations after your return.

What makes AYUVOYAGE different from other medical tourism platforms?

We combine traditional Ayurvedic expertise with modern analytics to match you with the most suitable centers. Our data-driven approach personalizes recommendations based on your goals, budget, and preferences.
